Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Perfect Sleep Time?

Simply select your desired wake-up time or bedtime, and the app will calculate the optimal times for you to go to bed or wake up, based on the 90-minute sleep cycle theory. You can also set reminders to help you stick to your sleep schedule.

Does the app collect any personal data?

No. Perfect Sleep Time does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Please make sure you have allowed notifications for Perfect Sleep Time in your device settings. The app only uses local notifications and does not require an internet connection.

Can I use the app without an account?

Yes! No account or sign-up is required. All features are available without registration.

We welcome your feedback! Please see the contact section below to get in touch.
