App Privacy

1. No Data Collection

Perfect Sleep Time does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users. All information, including your sleep preferences, reminder settings, and app configurations, is saved locally on your device. The app does not require you to create an account, does not use any form of analytics, and does not connect to any external servers.

2. No Tracking or Third-Party Sharing

  • No Tracking: This app does not track you across other apps or websites. There are no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or any third-party tracking tools integrated into the app.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with any third parties. All features are designed to work entirely offline, ensuring your privacy is always protected.

3. Local Data Storage Only

All user data, including your selected sleep times, notification preferences, and app settings, is stored exclusively on your device. None of this information is uploaded, transmitted, or synchronized to any cloud service or external server. If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently removed from your device.

4. No Sensitive Data or Identifiers

  • No Contact Information: The app does not request, collect, or store your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information.
  • No Health or Fitness Data: The app does not access or store any health or fitness data from your device or Apple Health.
  • No Location Data: The app does not request or use your location at any time.
  • No Device Identifiers: The app does not collect device identifiers or any information that could be used to identify you or your device.

5. Notification Permissions

Perfect Sleep Time only requests permission to send you local notifications. These notifications are used solely to remind you of your chosen sleep or wake-up times. Notification permissions are optional and can be managed at any time in your device settings. No notification data is sent off your device.

6. No Account Required

You can use all features of Perfect Sleep Time without creating an account or providing any personal information. There is no sign-up, login, or registration process.

7. No Analytics or Advertising

  • No Analytics: The app does not use any analytics services to monitor your usage or behavior.
  • No Advertising: There are no ads, ad networks, or marketing SDKs in the app. Your experience is completely ad-free.

Summary

Perfect Sleep Time is designed with your privacy in mind. No data is collected, tracked, or shared. All information stays on your device, and you are always in control.
